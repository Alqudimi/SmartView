# User Guide

This guide explains how to use the Smart View application from both the TV (Receiver) and Smartphone (Sender) perspectives.

## 1. Receiver Mode (Smart TV / Android Box)

The Receiver mode turns your device into a wireless display sink.

### Starting the Server
1. Open the application and select **TV (Receiver)**.
2. Tap the large **Power Icon** in the center of the screen.
3. The server will initialize the WiFi Direct network. Once it says **"Ready"**, your device is broadcasting.

### Settings & Security
Tap the **Settings** icon at the bottom left to configure:
- **Device Name:** Change how your TV appears to other devices.
- **Require PIN Connection:** Enable this to force senders to enter a 4-digit code before casting.
- **Sync Hotspot:** Automatically toggle your device's WiFi Hotspot when the receiver starts.

### Viewing a Stream
Once a device connects, the screen will automatically transition to the playback view. 
- Tap the screen to reveal the control overlay.
- Use the **Mute/Unmute** and **Play/Pause** buttons.
- To disconnect a sender, tap the **Stop (Square)** button and confirm.

---

## 2. Sender Mode (Smartphone)

The Sender mode allows you to cast your screen to a running Smart View Receiver.

### Connecting to a Display
1. Open the application and select **Phone (Sender)**.
2. The app will automatically scan for nearby Smart View receivers.
3. Tap on a device (e.g., `SmartTV-LivingRoom`) from the list.

### Quick Connect (QR Code)
1. Tap the **Scan QR Code** button at the bottom of the sender screen.
2. Point your camera at the QR code displayed on the Receiver's screen.
3. The app will parse the WiFi credentials and connect instantly.

### Stopping the Cast
While casting is active, your phone will display the "Casting Active" screen with a timer.
- Tap **Stop Casting** to terminate the connection and return to the discovery screen.
